Unvired Mobile Platform (UMP) mobilizes business processes and data from various enterprise backend systems to all leading mobile devices (smart phones and tablets). Mobilize Business Dashboards, Sales, Distribution, Plant Maintenance, Retail, Production, Customer Relationship Management, Finance, HR and other processes using UMP. Create beautiful mobile applications on iPhone, iPad, Android, BlackBerry, Windows Tablet and Phone. Connect to SAP, Oracle EBS, Microsoft Sharepoint, and many more on-premise and hosted enterprise systems.
